9. AI Processing and Third-Party AI Services

9.1. AI Processing Overview

TipsyPal uses artificial intelligence to provide writing assistance, text correction, style transformation, and content generation. To deliver these AI-powered features, your text inputs are processed by advanced language models.

9.2. OpenAI Integration

We currently utilize OpenAI's API services (including GPT models) to process your text inputs and generate AI responses. When you use AI features in TipsyPal:

  • Your text input is securely transmitted to OpenAI's servers via encrypted HTTPS connections
  • OpenAI processes your text to generate the requested output (correction, transformation, suggestion, etc.)
  • The AI-generated response is returned to TipsyPal and displayed to you
  • Both your input and the AI output may be stored in your conversation history

9.3. Data Protection in AI Processing

We have implemented the following safeguards for AI processing:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted to and from AI services is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • No Model Training: According to OpenAI's enterprise API terms, your data submitted via our API is NOT used to train or improve OpenAI's models
  • Data Minimization: We only send the minimum necessary text content to the AI service
  • No Sensitive Identifiers: We do not send your name, email, payment information, or device identifiers to AI services
  • Retention Limits: OpenAI retains API data for a maximum of 30 days for abuse monitoring, after which it is deleted
  • Contractual Protections: We have data processing agreements in place with our AI providers

9.4. OpenAI's Data Processing

OpenAI's processing of your data is governed by their API Data Usage Policies. Key points include:

  • API data is not used to train OpenAI's models
