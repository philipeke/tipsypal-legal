Your Privacy Rights: TipsyPal is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Swedish Data Protection Act, and international privacy standards. You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

1. Your Right to Erasure

Under GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure/"Right to be Forgotten") and similar provisions in international privacy laws, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. OakDev & AI AB respects this right and provides clear mechanisms for account and data deletion.

Upon receiving and verifying your deletion request, we will permanently delete your TipsyPal account and all associated personal data from our active systems, subject only to limited legal retention requirements as described below.

2. How to Request Account Deletion

2.1. Deletion Methods

You can request account deletion through the following methods:

Method 1: Email Request
  1. Send an email to hello@oakdev.app from the email address associated with your TipsyPal account
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – TipsyPal"
  3. In the email body, clearly state that you wish to permanently delete your TipsyPal account and all associated data
  4. Include your TipsyPal user ID or account email if different from the sending address
  5. We will respond to confirm receipt and provide a deletion confirmation within 30 days
Method 2: In-App Deletion (if available)
  1. Open the TipsyPal app and navigate to Settings
  2. Select Account or Privacy & Data
  3. Choose "Delete Account"
  4. Review the deletion confirmation dialog carefully
  5. Confirm deletion (this action is permanent and cannot be undone)
  6. You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

2.2. Identity Verification

Security Requirement: For your protection, account deletion requests must be verified. Requests submitted via email must originate from the email address linked to your TipsyPal account. If you no longer have access to your registered email, please contact us with alternative verification information (such as purchase receipts, user ID, or other account details).

We may request additional verification if:

  • The request comes from an email address not associated with the account
  • There are security concerns or unusual account activity
  • Multiple accounts are associated with the same email
  • Recent high-value purchases require fraud prevention checks

3. Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your deletion request has been verified and processed, the following data will be permanently deleted or irreversibly anonymized:

Data Category Description
Account Information Email address, display name, user ID, password hash, authentication tokens, account creation date
User-Generated Content All text inputs you provided to TipsyPal, prompts, messages, and conversation data
AI-Generated Content All AI outputs, responses, and generated text associated with your account
Conversation History Complete chat history, conversation metadata, timestamps, and interaction logs
Usage Data Credit usage records, feature usage patterns, session data, and app interaction logs
Device Information Device identifiers, push notification tokens, app installation IDs linked to your account
Analytics Data Personal analytics and usage statistics associated with your user ID
Preferences & Settings App preferences, language settings, notification preferences, accessibility settings

4. Data That May Be Retained

In compliance with legal obligations under Swedish and EU law, certain data may be retained for limited periods even after account deletion. This retention is necessary to satisfy mandatory legal, accounting, tax, and regulatory requirements.

4.1. Legally Required Retention

Data Type Retention Period Legal Basis
Purchase Records Up to 7 years Swedish tax and accounting laws (Bokföringslagen)
Transaction History Up to 7 years Tax compliance, VAT regulations
Subscription Records Duration of legal disputes Defense of legal claims, consumer protection compliance
Fraud Prevention Data As needed (de-identified) Fraud prevention, security, abuse prevention

4.2. Important Clarifications

  • Purchase records include transaction IDs, purchase dates, product SKUs, and amounts paid (but not credit card details, which we never store)
  • Retained data is secured with strict access controls and is never used for marketing, profiling, or service improvement
  • De-identified data (data that can no longer identify you) may be retained for fraud prevention or aggregate analytics
  • Backup systems may contain data for up to 90 days before being automatically overwritten
  • Third-party retention: Apple and Google may retain purchase data according to their own policies; contact them directly for deletion from their systems

5. Processing Timeline

Standard Processing Time: All verified account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt, in compliance with GDPR requirements. In most cases, deletion is completed within 3-7 days.

What to Expect:

  1. Immediate: Upon submitting your request, your account access may be immediately suspended
  2. Within 24-48 hours: You will receive an acknowledgment email confirming we received your request
  3. Within 3-7 days: Active data deletion is typically completed
  4. Within 30 days: You will receive final confirmation that your account and data have been deleted
  5. Within 90 days: Data is removed from backup systems during routine backup cycles

If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact us at hello@oakdev.app to inquire about the status of your request.

6. Consequences of Account Deletion

Permanent Action: Account deletion is irreversible and permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered or restored. Please ensure you have saved any important data before requesting deletion.

Upon account deletion, you will permanently lose:

  • All conversation history: Past AI interactions, saved messages, and generated content
  • Remaining credits: Unused AI credits from purchases or subscriptions (no refunds provided)
  • Tier access: Basic or Champion tier benefits and entitlements
  • Account preferences: All settings, customizations, and preferences
  • Account history: Usage statistics, achievements, or milestones

Additional Important Notes:

  • Subscriptions must be canceled separately: Deleting your TipsyPal account does NOT automatically cancel app store subscriptions. You must cancel subscriptions through your Apple or Google account to avoid future charges.
  • No refunds for deleted accounts: Deletion does not entitle you to refunds for unused subscription time or credits, except as required by law or app store policy.
  • Creating a new account: If you wish to use TipsyPal again, you must create a new account. Previous data, purchases, and credits cannot be transferred or restored.

7. Canceling App Store Subscriptions

To avoid continued billing, you must cancel subscriptions separately from account deletion:

For iOS (Apple) Users:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select Subscriptions
  4. Find and select TipsyPal
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
  6. Confirm cancellation

For Android (Google Play) Users:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap Menu (☰) > Subscriptions
  3. Select TipsyPal
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm
